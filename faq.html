Frequently Asked Questions

How does your online laundry service work ?

Our service is simple! You can place an order through our website, select your laundry preferences, and schedule a pickup. Our team will collect, wash, iron, and deliver your clothes back to your home within the promised time.

What types of laundry services do you offer?

We offer washing, drying, ironing, dry cleaning, and special fabric care services. You can also request stain removal and delicate fabric handling.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, mobile payments, and cash on delivery. Secure online transactions are processed through trusted payment gateways.

Do you clean traditional Bangladeshi clothes like sarees and panjabis?

Yes! We specialize in saree, panjabi, shalwar kameez, sherwani, and other delicate fabrics with proper care.

Do you have any discounts or offers?

Yes! We offer special discounts for first-time users, bulk orders, and monthly subscriptions. Follow us on Facebook and Instagram for updates on the latest offers.

Which areas in Bangladesh do you provide service to?

Currently, we offer services in Uttara, and we are expanding to more area soon!

How do I track my order?

You can track your laundry order in real-time through your account on our website. We will also send updates via email or SMS.
